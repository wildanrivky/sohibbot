# Website Audit — Checklist Reference

This is the detailed checklist used during the audit. Each item should be verified for every page crawled.

## Content & Copy Checklist

| # | Check | What to look for |
|---|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | Lorem ipsum | Any instance of "lorem ipsum", "dolor sit amet", or placeholder Latin text |
| 2 | Placeholder data | Zeroed stats (0, 0%, +0), "Name here", "Title here", "Description", "TBD", "Coming soon" without context |
| 3 | Spelling errors | Typos, missing accents, wrong characters |
| 4 | Grammar errors | Wrong verb conjugation, missing prepositions, subject-verb disagreement, wrong gender agreement |
| 5 | Dialect consistency | PT-BR vs PT-PT (e.g. "objectivo" vs "objetivo", "equipa" vs "time", "tu" vs "você"), US vs UK English (e.g. "color" vs "colour", "realize" vs "realise") |
| 6 | Tone consistency | Does every page sound like the same brand? Does the about page match the homepage? |
| 7 | Repetition | Same sentence or phrase used on multiple pages unintentionally |
| 8 | Weak copy | Vague value propositions, generic statements that could apply to any competitor |
| 9 | Incomplete content | Sections with headings but no body, pages that feel half-finished |
| 10 | Orphaned text | Floating phrases with no clear context or parent section |
| 11 | Inconsistent terminology | Same concept called different names on different pages |
| 12 | Numbers consistency | Stats that contradict each other across pages (e.g. "1.400 horas" on one page, "1.200 horas" on another) |

## UX & Structure Checklist

| # | Check | What to look for |
|---|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | Hero clarity | Does the hero communicate what the product is, who it's for, and what to do next within 5 seconds? |
| 2 | Self-selection | Can a visitor quickly identify which path/product/tier is for them? |
| 3 | Page narrative | Does the page tell a story that builds toward conversion? Or is it a random collection of sections? |
| 4 | Section order | Are sections arranged in a logical persuasion sequence? (Problem → Solution → Proof → Offer → Objections → CTA) |
| 5 | Cognitive overload | Any section trying to communicate more than 3 ideas at once? |
| 6 | Redundancy | Elements that appear twice on the same page without clear reason |
| 7 | Missing FAQ | Is there a FAQ addressing common objections? (Critical for products over $50/R$200) |
| 8 | Missing "How it works" | Is the process explained simply for first-time visitors? |
| 9 | Missing final CTA | Does the page end with a clear conversion moment before the footer? |
| 10 | Navigation clarity | Can you reach any page from any other page in 2 clicks or fewer? |
| 11 | Back-to-top / sticky nav | On long pages, can you navigate without endless scrolling? |
| 12 | Mobile text length | Are paragraphs short enough for mobile screens? (3-4 lines max per paragraph) |

## Conversion Checklist

| # | Check | What to look for |
|---|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | CTA clarity | Does every button tell you what happens when you click it? ("Falar com a equipe" vs "Falar com a equipe no WhatsApp") |
| 2 | CTA consistency | Does the same action use the same label everywhere? |
| 3 | Pricing visibility | Can a visitor find the price without excessive scrolling? |
| 4 | Price anchoring | Is there a comparison point that makes the price feel reasonable? (Higher-tier option, daily cost breakdown, competitor comparison) |
| 5 | Social proof placement | Are testimonials positioned after value prop and before pricing? |
| 6 | Stats credibility | Are numbers specific and verifiable? Round numbers feel made up. |
| 7 | Urgency signals | If scarcity is shown ("3 vagas restantes"), is it dynamic/real or static/fake? |
| 8 | Guarantee visibility | Is the refund/guarantee policy mentioned before asking for money? |
| 9 | Objection handling | Are the top 3-5 concerns a prospect would have addressed on the page? |
| 10 | Exit intent | Is there a newsletter, free resource, or soft CTA for visitors who aren't ready to buy? |

## Technical Checklist

| # | Check | What to look for |
|---|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | Broken links | Links that return 404 or redirect to wrong pages |
| 2 | Orphan pages | Pages not reachable from navigation |
| 3 | Duplicate content | Same content block rendered multiple times (common in Framer responsive variants) |
| 4 | Page titles | Does each page have a unique, descriptive <title> tag? |
| 5 | Meta descriptions | Are they present and unique per page? |
| 6 | Alt text quality | Do images have descriptive alt text? Are any missing? |
| 7 | Alt text accuracy | Do alt texts match what the image actually shows? (e.g. "man in black shirt" for a woman's photo) |
| 8 | Consistent URLs | Are URL slugs clean and descriptive? Any with encoded characters or typos? |
| 9 | Open Graph / social sharing | If you share the URL on social media, does it show the right title, description, and image? |
| 10 | Console errors | Any visible JavaScript errors or broken resources? |

## Brand & Creative Checklist

| # | Check | What to look for |
|---|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | Voice consistency | Does every page sound like the same brand wrote it? |
| 2 | Visual hierarchy | Are headings, subheadings, and body text used consistently? |
| 3 | Naming consistency | Are products, features, and programmes called the same thing everywhere? |
| 4 | Instructor/team completeness | Does every team member have a complete profile? (Name, role, company, bio, photo) |
| 5 | Photography consistency | Do images look like they belong to the same brand, or is it a mix of styles? |
| 6 | Pacing | Is there a rhythm to the page? Short sections followed by long, or is everything the same weight? |
| 7 | Emotional arc | Does the page make you feel something? Or is it purely informational? |

## Common PT-BR vs PT-PT Issues

For sites in Portuguese, pay special attention to these common dialect markers:

| PT-PT (European) | PT-BR (Brazilian) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| objectivo | objetivo | No "c" before "t" in PT-BR |
| activo | ativo | Same rule |
| equipa | time | "Equipa" is never used in Brazil |
| telemóvel | celular | |
| autocarro | ônibus | |
| tu | você | PT-BR uses "você" predominantly |
| estás | está / você está | |
| os nossos | nossos | PT-BR drops the article before possessives in most contexts |
| ligação (for a video call) | chamada / sessão / mentoria | "Ligação" in PT-BR means phone call |
| idéia | ideia | Post-reform spelling (2009 agreement) |
| heróico | heroico | Post-reform: no accent on diphthongs "oi" and "ei" |
| projecto | projeto | No "c" before "t" |
